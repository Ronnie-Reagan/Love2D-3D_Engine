What Sucks
- vehicle/buggy driving feels like im driving hover-carts
- the physics arent true enough to reality it seems; i have never gotten into a death spiral or been able to completely stall when going vertically up at no power: the plane always levels out or stabalizes
- draw distance being a sphere around the player sucks; i cant see the ground when im above the draw distance
- terrain mipmapping seems too sgressive resulting in dumbed down renders that are well within sight
- terrain gameplay/render radius is directly resulting in a lock to approx 20fps, almost hardware a agnostic level
